## Quality & Safety Rules

- **Hard cap at 100/day.** LinkedIn flags ~150+. We stay safely under.
- **Three sending windows:** roughly 35 in the morning, 35 midday, 30 evening — Claude staggers automatically.
- **Personalize every message.** No two messages identical. Claude must vary opening line + contextual line.
- **Reply to every response within 24 hours** — even "thanks, not for me" gets a gracious one-liner.
- **No link in the very first line** — LinkedIn sometimes collapses message previews when a URL leads.
- **Never re-message someone already in the funnel.** Claude maintains a sent-list CSV.
- **If someone replies "stop" or "remove" — full suppression, no follow-up.**
- **Buy 40+ copies = raffle hook.** Never explain what the raffle is in the DM. Let them ask.
- **Voice check:** John's voice is measured. If a draft reads as hyped or salesy, regenerate it.
