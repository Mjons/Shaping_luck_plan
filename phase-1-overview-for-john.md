## The Campaign Cadence

### Salim's Side (Email)

Salim sends **6 emails** over 4 weeks to his OpenEXO list. The arc:

| Week                      | Emails   | What They Do                                                |
| ------------------------- | -------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Week 1** (Apr 21–25)    | 2 emails | Announce the book. Plant the webinar seed. Build curiosity. |
| **Week 2** (Apr 28–May 2) | 2 emails | Introduce John. Tease the app. Deepen the story.            |
| **Week 3** (May 5–9)      | 2 emails | Scarcity (600 cap). Last call on $20 book price.            |

After that, people who bought the book get a separate short sequence nudging them to register for the webinar. People who registered get a 3-day countdown (May 16–18). Light touch — no one gets overwhelmed.

**Total emails any one person receives: 6–10 max** depending on what they do.

### John's Side (LinkedIn DMs)

John sends **1–2 personalized DMs** to his LinkedIn connections starting May 5.

- **Message 1:** Personal, contextual. Introduces the book and the May 19 session. Asks them to pass it along to 2–3 people who'd get something out of it.
- **Message 2:** Only if they opened but didn't reply. Lighter. Adds scarcity (X/600 spots left).

Each message is tailored to who the person is — not a blast. We have contextual templates for different types of connections (fellow authors, founders, execs, former collaborators, etc.).

**Optionally:** 2–3 public LinkedIn posts from John in the 10 days before the webinar to catch anyone the DMs missed.

## The Webinar — May 19

This is the conversion event. Everything before it builds to this moment.

**Format:** 90 minutes. Salim + John live on camera together for the first time.

**Structure:**

| Time          | What Happens                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **0–20 min**  | Connection. Why we wrote the book. The framework. A real story from testing.                                                                                                                                        |
| **20–28 min** | The reveal. Show the app. **Drop the price: $400 lifetime.** Let it land.                                                                                                                                           |
| **28–70 min** | The stack. For 40 minutes, keep adding value on top of the $400. Matching demo. Real stories. Salim avatar demo. Meaning of Life sessions. Roadmap. The audience spends 40 minutes thinking "all of this for $400?" |
| **70–75 min** | Risk reversal. Full refund guarantee. 600-person cap. Deadline: offer closes when the session ends.                                                                                                                 |
| **75–90 min** | Q&A + close. Salim handles vision questions, John handles "does this work" questions. Links stay live.                                                                                                              |

**Key move:** Price drops early (minute 25), not late. Then we spend 40 minutes stacking value. The audience never sits through a pitch wondering "how much?" — they already know, and every new feature makes $400 feel more absurd.

## After the Webinar

**2 follow-up emails** go out to everyone who didn't convert:

1. **Replay + offer recap** — sent the day after
2. **"Doors closing tonight"** — final close, then it's over

Anyone who still doesn't convert moves to an **evergreen nurture**: 1 email, pure value, no selling. Keeps the relationship warm for Phase 2.
